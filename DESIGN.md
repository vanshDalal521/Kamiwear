# Design System: Kamiwear
**Project ID:** 3426749929440354387

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The Kamiwear aesthetic is "Anime-Techwear Cinematic." It combines high-energy Shōnen visuals with Silicon Valley glassmorphism. The vibe is premium, mysterious, and high-performance, utilizing deep backgrounds, vibrant accent highlights, and translucent layers.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Electric Primary Yellow (#f2df0d):** The "Bolt" color. Used for primary call-to-action buttons, high-priority badges, and critical interactive highlights.
* **Deep Midnight Purple (#3c1a5b):** The "Shadow" color. Used as the foundation for gradients and deep background elements.
* **Radiant Purple Light (#7c3aed):** The "Energy" color. Used for secondary accents, highlight borders, and glow effects.
* **Near Black (#121212):** The primary background color for the dark theme.
* **Glass White (#ffffff0d):** Used for panel backgrounds to create depth with backdrop blur.
* **Glass Border (#ffffff1a):** Subtle borders for panels and sections.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings (Syne):** Extra bold, uppercase, often italicized with tight tracking. Used for page titles and section headers to create a "strike" impact.
* **Body (Space Grotesk):** Clean, geometric, and modern. Used for all primary content, descriptions, and functional text.
* **Letter Spacing:** Wide tracking for labels and narrow tracking for headlines.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Pill-shaped (rounded-full) with high-contrast text. Primary buttons use the Electric Yellow background.
* **Cards/Containers (Glass Panels):** Generously rounded corners (1rem to 3rem). Backgrounds use a low-opacity white with a heavy backdrop blur (12px) and a subtle 1px border.
* **Inputs:** Dark translucent backgrounds with no-border styles and primary yellow focus rings.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Generous vertical spacing between sections (padding 24).
* **Alignment:** Center-aligned heroes with grid-based content sections below.
* **Navigation:** Fixed top navigation with a glassmorphic background to maintain brand presence.

Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return and exchange policy

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Final sale items cannot be returned
  • Undergarments and swimwear are final sale for hygiene reasons

Return Process:

  1. Initiate your return through our Online Returns Portal
  2. Print the prepaid return label and packing slip
  3. Package your return securely and attach the label
  4. Drop off at any authorized shipping center

Refund Information:

  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

Exchange Policy

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability. Follow the same return process and indicate you'd like an exchange. If the replacement item costs more, you'll be charged the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

Important Notes

For international returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs fees. We recommend using a trackable shipping service as we cannot be responsible for lost return packages.

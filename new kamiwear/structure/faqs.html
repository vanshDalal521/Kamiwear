Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within India and 7-14 business days for international orders. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and customs processing. During peak seasons, please allow additional time for delivery.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and select digital wallets. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders within India with a nominal additional charge.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can track your order by:

  • Logging into your account on our website
  • Using the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer support with your order number

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Please visit our Returns & Exchanges page for detailed instructions. Note that certain items like undergarments, swimwear, and sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.

We provide detailed size charts for all our products on each product page. You can also refer to our comprehensive Size Guide for general measurements and fitting advice. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery to your doorstep

Shipping Options

We offer multiple shipping methods to suit your needs

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 3-5 business days (India), 7-14 business days (International)

Cost: Free for orders over ₹2000 | ₹99 for orders under ₹2000

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 1-2 business days (India), 3-5 business days (International)

Cost: ₹199 (India) | ₹499 (International)

International Shipping

We ship worldwide to most countries. International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes which are the responsibility of the customer. These charges vary by country and we have no control over them.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). Orders placed after 2PM IST will be processed the next business day.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order through our website or the carrier's tracking system.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently cannot ship to PO boxes or military addresses. For security reasons, we require a physical address for all deliveries.

Important Note

Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Factors like weather, customs delays, and carrier issues may affect delivery times. During peak seasons, processing and delivery times may be extended.

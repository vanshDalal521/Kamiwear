# Kamiwear — Site Vision & Roadmap

## 1. Project Overview

**Kamiwear** is a premium anime-themed e-commerce store selling hoodies, t-shirts, and accessories. The design language combines **Liquid Glass Minimalism** with bold anime culture aesthetics. Think Apple-level polish meets Akihabara energy.

**Brand Colors:**
- Deep Purple `#3C1A5B` — soul of the brand
- Electric Yellow `#FFF748` — action, CTAs
- Near Black `#0A0812` — dark backgrounds
- Soft Lavender `#F2EDFF` — light backgrounds

**Design Style:** Liquid glass cards (frosted backdrop-filter), generous whitespace, Syne display font, Inter body text, Orbitron for timers/badges.

**Device:** DESKTOP-first (responsive to mobile)

**Theme:** Both Light and Dark mode supported (dark is primary for generation)
